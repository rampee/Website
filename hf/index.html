I'm interested
01

Improving movement

If you put your body in the right positions, you are stronger and less likely to become injured; I teach my clients how to move more efficiently, and in ways that take less of a toll on their bodies. If you move better, you feel better doing it, and you’re more likely to do it again.

02

Achieving balance

I believe in balance when it comes to nutrition--if a diet preaches completely cutting things out, it’s probably not sustainable; instead I try to eat a lot of veggies, watch out for portions that are too large, enjoy the bad-for-you foods I love in moderation. I use soft-tissue techniques to manage tension imbalances that lead to compensatory movement patterns.

03

Preventing injury

Before I became a personal trainer, I worked in a physical therapy clinic for 2.5 years; this helped me understand how professionals manage rehabilitation. Having been a competitive athlete, I learned how soft-tissue techniques could allow me to train harder and more frequently--especially as I’ve gotten older and continue to chase improvement.

I believe in a
wide holistic approach to fitness.

In my 23 years of working out and coaching others, I’ve accumulated extensive experience that I can use to help you be the best you can be: from becoming stronger, to injury management and avoidance, to improving your posture.
